What Are the SDGs?

The SDGs (Sustainable Development Goals) are 17 global goals made by the UN (United Nations) to achieve by 2030.
These goals were made in the year 2000 as a retry to the MDGs (Millennium Development Goals) that were 8 goals made to be achieved by the year 2015.
